Welcome to Velocity Performance Strength and Conditioning

We are on a mission to help aspiring college athletes as well as current college athletes strength train, speed train, prevent injury, and promote overall success in their sport! We prepare our athletes to not only train at their current level, but to train at the next level as well!

Book a Session with Us!

Here's how to book:

  1. Browse our various services listed on our "SERVICES" page.
  2. Choose the training program that fits your sport and goals.
  3. Visit our "CONTACT" page and send us your information.
  4. One of our trainers will reach out to schedule your first session!
